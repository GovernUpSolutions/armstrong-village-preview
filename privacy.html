Privacy

Last updated June 28, 2026

This page explains how the Armstrong Village Homeowners Association website handles information. It applies to this public website only. TownSq, Facebook, email providers, and other linked services have their own privacy practices.

Information this website collects

This static website does not include account registration, payment processing, comment forms, analytics scripts, advertising trackers, or cookies set by Armstrong Village HOA.

If the site is hosted online, the website host may automatically process standard technical information, such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages requested, and access times, as part of ordinary web hosting and security operations.

Information you choose to send

Some links open an email message to the board, property manager, or service provider. If you send an email, the information you include is received by the email recipients and may be used to respond to your request, document an HOA matter, or route the issue to the appropriate party.

Third-party links

This site links to services used by residents and the community, including:

  • TownSq for resident account access, official notices, requests, and HOA records.
  • The Armstrong Village Facebook group for neighbor-to-neighbor community updates.
  • Email links for the board, CMC/Associa, and Level Green Landscaping contacts.

When you leave this website, the privacy policy of the third-party service applies.

Resident records

Account-specific information, assessments, maintenance requests, official notices, and HOA records should be handled through TownSq or the appropriate HOA management channel, not through this public website.

Questions

For privacy questions about this website, contact the board at armstrongvillagehoa@gmail.com.
