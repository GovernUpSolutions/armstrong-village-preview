Our Community

Board service, clear information, and a neighborhood that works.

Armstrong Village is a townhome community in Upper Marlboro near major commuter routes, Joint Base Andrews, and the Beltway. This website is intended to keep practical information easy to find for homeowners and residents.

292 total units 204 original homes plus 88 WestEnd units through annexation.
Managed by CMC Associa Community Management Corporation supports day-to-day operations.
TownSq platform The secure resident portal remains the official account and request hub.
Reserve planning A full reserve study was completed in November 2025.
